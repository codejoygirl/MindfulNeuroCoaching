Why Mindful NeuroCoaching?

Our Head Coach, Grace Reynolds, is a Trauma-Informed Relationship NeuroCoach, who is passionate about transforming societies, one step at a time.
After a childhood of continued trauma, Grace now educates her clients how to use the latest brain and evidence-based strategies from Network Neuroscience, Relaxed Mindful Awareness and her spiritual wisdom to empower her clients to clarify and live who they truly are, at their core and spiritual essence.
This is particularly helpful after trauma, because her clients have the benefit of not only her own life experiences but they can also achieve their own outcomes as they transcend post traumatic growth in the following five ways;
Personal Strength (no more interruptions to your day from unwieldy emotions & memory traces)
Relating to Others (learning a new Compassionate Communication Strategy that resolves conflict and allows the engager and listener to be part of a discussion without emotional interference);
New Possibilities: Once we have recovered our values system, restored our core selves, we then rebuild ourselves in ways that we may not have considered were holding us back or keeping us stuck in patterns that were not helpful for new life and abundant living...

...Why Mindful NeuroCoaching?

Appreciation of Life: Our final level is rejoicing over who we are. Gratitude for this level of faith and belief in ourselves causesreal joy to be a lived experience, day by day.
Spiritual and Existential Freedom: Once we have completed our course, we are living our lives in true authenticity, agency and abundance.
Grace has years of practical experience and many success stories to back up herlegendary reputation in this area.
She has served in Australia and internationally as a School, Prison, and Acute Psychiatric Hospital Chaplain, has worked with refugees, displaced and indigenous populations, war veterans, drug and alcohol abuse, youth at risk, child and adult sexual abuse, family violence, victims of crime, anti-human trafficking, workers’ compensation systems, conflictresolution, and moral injury.
Most of her clients over the years have been entrepreneurs and leaders in society, learning to master their own lives so that they can flourish because they know who they are and live from their whole and powerful souls.
